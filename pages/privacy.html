Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ocean Breeze Luxury Apartments ("we", "us", "our") operates mrafrica12.github.io/template1 and is based in the United States with its physical property located in Bijilo, The Gambia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a booking.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a booking inquiry or reservation, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Nationality
  • Travel dates and apartment preference
  • Number of guests and any special requests

We also collect standard website analytics data (pages visited, device type, browser) through aggregated, anonymous statistics. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your booking
  • To send you a booking confirmation email
  • To contact you about your reservation via email or WhatsApp
  • To improve our website and guest experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Booking information is stored securely in Google Sheets, hosted on Google's infrastructure, and is accessible only to authorised Ocean Breeze staff. We retain booking records for up to 3 years for accounting and dispute-resolution purposes, after which they are deleted.

4. Email Communications

When you complete a booking, we will send a single confirmation email to the address you provided. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If you contact us, we may reply to your inquiry. You can request removal from any communication at any time by emailing oceanbreezegambia@yahoo.com.

5. Payment Information

We do not store credit card or payment account details on our servers. Payment arrangements are made directly with our team via bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), or PayPal. Any payment processors you use are subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to any legal retention requirements)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at oceanbreezegambia@yahoo.com with the subject line "Data Request".

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use Google Fonts, which may load fonts from Google's servers — please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
